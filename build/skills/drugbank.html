Medication Service

A Go service that uses DrugBank to provide information about medications.

Terminology Matching

A microservice written in Python that matches medical terminology between medical ontologies, like SNOMED CT, ICD-10, DrugBank and RXNorm. The service used a fuzzy matching algorithms along with a graph-based approach (NetworkX) to find the most similar concepts.
